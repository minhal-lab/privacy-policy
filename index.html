Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 13, 2026

Word Master Challenge ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

Data stored locally on your device:

  • Player name (set by you in Settings)
  • High scores and game statistics
  • App preferences (sound, difficulty)

This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

2. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. This may include:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Usage data

For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except as described in the Advertising section above.

4. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require account creation or personal data input beyond an optional player name stored locally.

5. Internet Permission

The app requires internet access solely for loading advertisements. Gameplay works fully offline.

6. Data Security

All game data is stored locally on your device using standard Android storage mechanisms. No data is transmitted to external servers by the app itself.

7. Your Rights

You can clear all app data at any time through your device's Settings > Apps > Word Master Challenge > Clear Data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
